About Us

Welcome to the Email Spam Classifier application! Our goal is to help you identify and classify spam emails using advanced machine learning techniques. Here is a brief overview of what our application does and its features:

Features

  • Email Content Analysis: Enter the content of an email to check if it's spam or not.
  • File Upload: Upload a text file containing email content to classify it.
  • Real-time Classification: Get instant results on whether an email is spam or not.
  • Data Visualization: Visualize the classification results with intuitive charts.

About Machine Learning

Our application uses a Naive Bayes classifier, a popular machine learning algorithm, to analyze and classify email content. The model has been trained on a large dataset of labeled emails, allowing it to accurately predict whether a new email is spam or not.

Examples

To clarify, here are two email examples, one spam and one non-spam:
Spam Email:
Congratulations! You have won a free vacation to the Bahamas. Click here to claim your prize. Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity!
Keywords: "Congratulations", "free vacation", "Click here", "prize".
Suspicious links.

Not-Spam Email:
Hello team,
Just a reminder about the meeting tomorrow at 10 AM. We will discuss the project updates and next steps. Please make sure to bring all necessary documents.
Best regards, TDat.
Keywords: "reminder", "meeting", "project updates".
Professional language and no suspicious requests for action.
